Dr.Drew's City Escape




You are Dr.Drew, a criminal mastermind you must use his drawing abilities to escape the forces of justice.
Gameplay and Controls:

  • Use the side arrow keys to move side to side
  • Up arrow key to jump
  • Space bar to draw bridges into the environment
  • Don't fall off the stage - you'll die
  • Also, don't let Police Cheif Rhombus touch you - you'll die
  • Reach the end token at the end of each stage as fast as you can.
  • And lastly, don't go over your shape limit every level, or you'll be unable to draw your way out of trouble!
